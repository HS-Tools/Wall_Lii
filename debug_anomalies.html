Anomaly

From Hearthstone Wiki
< Battlegrounds
Jump to navigation Jump to search
Secrets of Norgannon, one of the most common Anomalies to appear

Anomaly is a special card type in Battlegrounds. Anomalies are game-changing effects that are randomly set at the start of each game.

Anomalies were first available in Battlegrounds Season 5.

Contents

  • 1 Notes
  • 2 Anomalies
    • 2.1 Unused Anomalies
  • 3 Gallery

Notes[edit | edit source]

  • All players in the game get the same Anomaly for that game.
  • Anomaly cards have a reddish purple spell frame, similar to but different from warlock's.
  • Anomaly appearance rates vary, with some more common than others.
  • Some minions, minion types, and heroes won't be offered if a particular Anomaly applies to your game.
  • You'll know your game's Anomaly when you're picking your hero.
